About Us

Rough Rider Racing (RRR) is the University of Ottawa Faculty of Engineering’s Baja SAE team. Baja SAE is an international competition organised by SAE International that challenges students to design and build a single‑seat utility terrain vehicle (UTV) built to withstand harsh off‑road conditions. There are three North American competitions annually, each with around 100 competing teams.

Our team resumed operations after the COVID‑19 pause and now consists of around 40 passionate students from mechanical, electrical, civil, software, computer and biomedical engineering. Through Baja SAE we gain hands‑on experience in design, fabrication, project management and teamwork – skills we bring back to the classroom and future careers. We are organised into four subteams: Admin, Electrical, Suspension and Chassis.
